Data Description for Alloy Test Dataset
==========================================

Sample tabular data files for testing the tabular2mcap conversion tool.

location.csv
------------
GPS location data with timestamp and error estimates (San Francisco area).
- timestamp_sec: Unix timestamp (float), 1698768000.0 to 1698768059.0 (1 Hz, 60 seconds)
- latitude: Decimal degrees (float), 37.7749° to 37.7808° (San Francisco)
- longitude: Decimal degrees (float), -122.4194° to -122.4135° (San Francisco)
- altitude: Meters above sea level (float), 25.5m to 43.2m
- latitude_error: Uncertainty in degrees (float), constant 0.0005°
- longitude_error: Uncertainty in degrees (float), constant 0.0005°
- altitude_error: Uncertainty in meters (float), constant 1.2m

Data shows gradual northeast movement with increasing altitude.

sensor.csv
----------
Sensor readings with timestamp and multiple measurements.
- timestamp_sec: Unix timestamp (float), 1698768000.0 to 1698768059.0 (synchronized with location.csv)
- pressure1: Atmospheric pressure in hPa (float), 1013.25 to 1015.02
- pressure2: Atmospheric pressure in hPa (float), 1013.30 to 1015.07
- sensor3: Temperature in °C (float), 23.5 to 29.4
- sensor4: Humidity in % (float), 45.2 to 51.1

All values show gradual increases over the 60-second time window.
